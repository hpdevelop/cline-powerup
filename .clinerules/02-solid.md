# SOLID Principles

**Single Responsibility**: Each module, class, or function has exactly one reason to change.
**Open/Closed**: Extend behavior by adding new code, not by modifying existing code.
**Liskov Substitution**: Subtypes must be substitutable for their base types without altering correctness.
**Interface Segregation**: Prefer small, focused interfaces. Do not force clients to depend on methods they do not use.
**Dependency Inversion**: Depend on abstractions, not concretions. High-level modules must not depend on low-level modules.
